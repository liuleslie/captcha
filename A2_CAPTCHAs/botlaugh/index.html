About laughCAPTCHA

laughCAPTCHA plays a target laugh and asks you to mimic it.

Your recording is compared locally — nothing is uploaded. We compare spectral shape (MFCCs via Meyda.js) and pitch using Dynamic Time Warping. Pass threshold: 50% similarity.

This challenge requires microphone access.
