About breathCAPTCHA

breathCAPTCHA verifies human presence by detecting a breath on your webcam lens. A brief fog pattern caused by exhaling alters the high-frequency energy of the camera image.

All analysis runs locally. The system measures spectral changes; it does not record or store video.
