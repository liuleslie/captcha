CAPTCHA Diary

Data collection consent

What this extension collects

When you visit a tab while the extension is active and a CAPTCHA is detected on that page, the extension automatically captures:

  • CAPTCHA images (challenge imagery, network-intercepted and DOM-extracted)
  • The page URL and hostname serving the CAPTCHA
  • Your cursor position as (x, y) coordinates over time
  • CAPTCHA position within the page (DOM selectors, bounding box)
  • Browser viewport dimensions and frame structure
All data stays in your browser. Collected sessions are stored in an in-browser archive (IndexedDB). Nothing is transmitted to any server. You can browse your archive, delete sessions, or export a ZIP at any time from the sidebar.

How monitoring works

Monitoring is automatic — no toolbar click required. When you visit a tab, the extension watches it for CAPTCHAs. Recording begins when a CAPTCHA element appears on the page and saves when it disappears or you navigate away.

Tabs you have not visited are never monitored, even if they load a CAPTCHA in the background.

Scope of this consent: This applies to this browser window for this browser session only. When you open a new window or restart the browser, you will be asked again.

If you decline: This window will not be monitored. You can open a new window and consent there, or revisit by opening a new window and clicking the toolbar button.
