Welcome to CAPTCHA Club.

CAPTCHA Club’s member numbers are mighty; any user who has completed a CAPTCHA (often colloquially known as the “I’m Not a Robot” test) is automatically enrolled into this Club. This means that Club members include both humans (working as human computers) and computer programs (bots, at times perhaps equipped with machine learning enhancements).
