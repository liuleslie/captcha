About heartCAPTCHA

heartCAPTCHA verifies human presence by detecting a heartbeat through your webcam. Place a fingertip over your camera lens — the system reads subtle color changes caused by blood flow.

All signal processing runs locally in your browser. No video or biometric data is transmitted or stored.

Based on HeartCam by Zijun Yang, Jingjia Peng, Mo Yang, and Yijun Wang (EECS 442).
